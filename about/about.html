Crafts of Coastal India — A Celebration

This short film explores traditional coastal handicrafts: coconut shell carving, terracotta potteries and handmade jewellery. Each piece is crafted by local artisans using age-old techniques passed down through generations. Learn about materials, process and the community that makes these pieces possible.

Quick Facts

  • Origin: Coastal villages (Kerala, Bengal)
  • Materials: Coconut shell, clay, reclaimed wood
  • Handmade: Each piece unique
